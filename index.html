About

Life is always moving and waiting for no one, one day we will have to leave this world, which is the law of nature. Life is not always what we want it to be, but we can choose how we face it and live a meaningful and valuable life.

COLABS was born with the aim of bringing practical STEM values to the world community. COLABS is a science and education project founded with the desire to bring people together and remove social prejudice barriers to inequality in science from all genders, ages and positions in order to create an international STEM academic community.
